The Origin Story

🌺
Honolulu, Hawaii
Where it all began
Year 1: Hersh and Michael meet at a wellness trade show — on opposite sides, selling competing products.
Day 3: End up at the same beach bar. Instant friendship.
Year 2: Both independently discover red light therapy — Mike for his back, Hersh for his shoulder.
One phone call: "We have to do something with this." BioGlow is born. 🔴

Hersh and Michael were never supposed to be partners. They were competitors at a trade show in Honolulu — both convinced their own approach was right.

Then the show floor closed, everyone ended up at the same beach bar, and somewhere between rounds they realized they shared one frustration: the most powerful wellness technologies in the world were locked behind walls most people couldn't get through.

Light therapy entered both their lives separately. Mike had a chronic lower back issue. Hersh had a rotator cuff his doctors said he'd "just have to live with." Red light therapy changed both of their lives.

They got on a call. "Did you know a Nobel Prize was awarded for this in 1903?" Neither had a good answer for why nobody knew. So they decided to do something about it.
