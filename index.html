Treatment Protocols

A protocol for
every condition

Step-by-step guides for every area of the body.

Lower Back & Hips
4x/Week · 15–20 Min
Slipped discs · Arthritis · Sciatica · Scoliosis
Start at the tailbone near L1-L2 and hold 3 minutes. Move up 2 min/inch to L5. Treat the Piriformis 4–5 minutes, then the psoas muscle 3–4 minutes. Light stretching after.
Knee Conditions
3–4x/Week · 15–20 Min
ACL · MCL · Meniscus · Arthritis · Bone on bone
Start on the Vastus Medialis 2–3 min. Move under and around the knee 2–3 min per inch. Finish behind the knee 3 minutes. Stretch and drink water after.
Foot Conditions
4x/Week · 15–20 Min
Plantar fasciitis · Neuropathy · Diabetes · Flat arch
Place under the arch 3–4 minutes. Move toward heel 2–3 min/inch. Hold under the balls of feet 3 minutes. Light foot stretches encouraged after.
Shoulder Conditions
4x/Week · 15–20 Min
Frozen shoulder · Rotator cuff · Arthritis
Start on the middle deltoid 4 minutes. Move to front deltoid, then rear deltoid. Light stretching recommended after each session until full mobility returns.
Anti-Aging Facial
Daily · 4–8 Min/Area
Wrinkles · Dark spots · Fine lines · Eczema
Start under the chin, massage up along the jawline. Take 3–4 minutes per half of the face. Add 5 minutes for the neck. Apply skincare routine after.
Hair Loss
Daily · 10–15 Min
Male & female pattern balding · Alopecia
Use BioGlow before applying minoxidil. Hold on balding area 3 minutes, treat every other surface 3 minutes until each area is covered.
Eyes & Headaches
4x/Week · 6–10 Min
Macular degeneration · Migraines · Anxiety
Hold 2–3 inches from closed eyes 3 minutes. Massage in circles over each temple 2–3 minutes. Keep eyelids closed throughout.
Autoimmune Disorders
Daily · 10–15 Min
Rheumatoid arthritis · Fibromyalgia · Lupus
Focus on areas of most tension. Hold 3–4 minutes on high-tension zones, massage in small circles 6–10 minutes. Drink 8–12 oz water after.
Elbow Conditions
3–4x/Week · 10–12 Min
Tennis elbow · Golfer's elbow · Nerve compression
Start on the inside of the elbow 4 minutes. Move 1 inch around at 2–3 minutes, repeating until you return to start. Light stretching after.
Hand Conditions
3–4x/Week · 9–13 Min
Carpal tunnel · Trigger finger · Arthritis
Start on the underside of the wrist 3 minutes. Move to the palm 3–4 minutes. Spend 3–5 more minutes between both areas. Lightly stretch hands to finish.
Neck Conditions
3–4x/Week · 7–10 Min
Nerve compression · Bulging discs · Stenosis
Place on C6-C7 (base of neck) 3 minutes. Move up 1 inch and hold 2–3 minutes until reaching the base of the head. Allow 24 hours between treatments.
Leg Conditions
4x/Week · 10–14 Min
Hamstring tear · Pulled muscles · IT band
Start under the glute 4 minutes. Move 2–3 min/inch to the bottom of the hamstring. For quads, start at hips and work down. Stretch after.
General Wellness & Chronic Conditions
Daily · 10–20 Min
Cardiovascular health · Organ function · Chronic inflammation
Use BioGlow daily on areas of concern. Cycle through all 4 modes for 1–2 minutes each. Always drink water after to support natural detoxification.
